Lima Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Site Name: Lima Chamber of Commerce

Reason for Name: The name is a direct representation of Lima as the capital of Peru and the hub for economic activity, business services, and community development.

Site Purpose

  • Provide a platform for businesses to promote services and network.
  • Highlight local business opportunities and regional events in Lima.

Scenarios

  • What networking events can I attend to connect with local businesses in Lima?
  • Where can I find support for starting a business in Lima?
  • What are the economic trends in Lima and how can I benefit from them?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #2E5A79 (used for headings, navigation bar)

Accent Color: #774936 (used for buttons, links)

Typography

Font for Headings: 'Roboto', sans-serif (applied to all h1, h2, and h3 elements)

Font for Body Text: 'Arial', sans-serif (applied to all paragraphs and general text)

Wireframe

Here is a rough sketch of the layout for the Lima Chamber of Commerce website:

  • Mobile View: Single column layout with a stacked header, main content, and footer.
  • Desktop View: Two-column layout with a sidebar for events, and main content for news and announcements.
